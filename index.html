The Time Inversion Audit

If someone audited your last 30 days and inferred what you were optimising for — purely from where your time went — what would they conclude?

Not what you'd tell them. What the evidence says. Answer both honestly and this will show you the gap between the outcome you claim and the outcome your hours are actually producing. Two minutes. No account.

What would you say you're building right now? Use your own words, including the vague ones.
Where did the hours actually go these last 30 days? Calendar, screen time, the evenings. Be unflattering — vague answers produce vague audits.
