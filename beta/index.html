What you'd be testing

O.R.A. isn't another to-do app or productivity tracker. It treats your outcome as something to be engineered, not willed into being. You don't need a manager for your time. You need an architect. It runs as a loop, in four stages:

Define
The app asks you a series of discovery questions to surface the outcome you actually want — the honest one, not the socially acceptable one.

Design
Engineer your environment so the right move becomes the path of least resistance.

Deliver
Run the smallest version of the system that still moves the needle — even on your worst week.

Verify
Each week, audit the architecture, not yourself. "Where did the system fail?" — never "Why did I fail?"
